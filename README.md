# Resourceful Refinement
Improved industrial refinement of your mineral resources through automation, metallurgy and progressively complex fluid management, all powered by Create!

![Resourceful Refinement Banner](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/9f04a36ea0bcf7376f445eddf0fbc05338d1af84_0.webp)

## **About:**
**Resourceful Refinement** is an addon for Create which builds upon the gameplay loop for obtaining mineral resources, increasing your production outputs, and automating large-scale industrial processes. Instead of relying on overly-optimised cobblestone generators or vanilla mob farms that don't require player engagement or infrastructure support, Resourceful Refinement provides an alternative source for your basic metals which is renewable, automatable, and progression orientated, making the process of constructing your factory a deep and fulfilling experience. Through managing new fluids and mechanical processing blocks, Resourceful Refinement helps to bridge the gap between Minecraft and factory-game experience inspired by _Satisfactory_ and _Factorio_.

Resourceful Refinement is an ongoing development process, that will slowly build upon its framework, and introduce new content and avenues for automating materials in unique ways over time. The aim is to design and implement new content while obeying three core principles; _Innovation_, _Versitility_, and _Effeciency_. 

This project is currently in _Alpha_. 



## **Content:**
### **Mechanical Blocks**
![Mechanical blocks](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/08ae56c27216dd5b443bf833a8a7b0fd78670316.png)
- **Mechanical Fluid Sieve**: Dredge by-products from fluids passing through the sieve. Stack sieves vertically to improve their output rates
- **Mechanical Forge Mould & Casting Depot**: Cast fluids directly into items on depots and belts
- **Blender Blade**: Spin entities around or passively damage them

### **Fluid Refinement**
![Multiblock structures](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/4d31993439efd64697ddc440f8a52a2b9c3e22a1.png)
- **Fracking Pylon**: Extract endless resources from geysers, by turning water and kinetic input into molten ores using powerful Fracking Pylons
- **Fluid Refinery**: Refine ores into efficient derivatives, or produce effective alloys and blends that beat mixer recipes in the Fluid Refinery
- **Molten Fluids**: Ore fluids progressively improve as you proceed along their refinement tree, from _molten_, to _catalysed_, to _purified_ fluids.

### **Geysers**
![Geyser block & fluid](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/46c7726d7390e2fc6e552c254b8baf93280203d6.png)
- Discover naturally spawning geysers as you explore the world, which you can manually harvest yourself, or exploit with a Fracking Pylon.
- Crimsite, Veridium, Ochrum, Asurine, Lava, and Carborax geysers spawn naturally in most overworld biomes or underground. Scorchia geysers can be found rarely throughout the nether.

### **Coatings**
![Coatings & recipe](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/c4037ae4d6aec0800c463a7036b5608150850144.png)
- Improve your tools and indefinitely extend their durability by using a Casting Depot and Forge Mould to coat your tools in molten fluids. Coatings act as a secondary durability bar, and each apply their own special effect while active.



## **Compatibility & Requirements:**

Resourceful Refinement is a _NeoForge_ mod for 1.21.1

**Requires** Create 6.0.8+ and JEI 19.27.0.336
 
 
**Known Incompatibilities:**
- _Create Aeronautics_ should be used with caution. Multiblock structures (like Fluid Refineries, Sieve stacks, or Fracking Pylons) built on existing simulated physics contraptions work fine with little to no issues. If simulated contraptions containing these are assembled or disassembled however, the affected multiblock structures are known to sometimes break and cause crashes or leave behind indestructible ghost blocks. In the future, I hope to address this.

If you encounter any bugs or crashes, please notify me and provide a crash log or steps to reproduce the issue.
