Many of the fluids added by this mod fall into distinct groups, which share similar properties and assets, and represent similar stages in progression. I've described each group and it's members in the sections below:

### Raw Molten Minerals
These are raw minerals in molten form, obtained from geysers or smelting raw ore.

**Flow distance (getAmount()):** 5
**Light Level:** 10
**Density:** 3000
**Viscosity:** 3000

**Members**
- Molten Crimsite
- Molten Ochrum
- Molten Veridium
- Molten Scorchia
- Molten Asurine


### Catalysed Fluids
These are fluids which have undergone some form of prior processing

**Flow distance (getAmount()):** 6
**Light Level:** 8
**Density:** 2000
**Viscosity:** 1500

**Members**
- Catalysed Iron
- Catalysed Gold
- Catalysed Copper
- Catalysed Zinc
- Catalysed Redstone
- Catalysed Carborax


### Alloyed Fluids
These are fluids which are comprised of multiple ingredients, and are often used as inputs into other recipes

**Flow distance (getAmount()):** 7
**Light Level:** 8
**Density:** 2000
**Viscosity:** 2500

**Members**
- Silica Substrate
- Molten Andesite Blend
- Molten Brass Blend
- Molten Netherite Blend
- Durasteel Alloy


### Purified Fluids
These are fluids which represent the end of their processing trees

**Flow distance (getAmount()):** 6
**Light Level:** 8
**Density:** 2000
**Viscosity:** 4000

**Members**
- Purified Iron
- Purified Gold
- Purified Copper
- Purified Zinc
- Purified Durasteel


### Carborax Fluids
These are fluids which have a burn time, like lava, and can be used as fuel sources.

**Flow distance (getAmount()):** 8
**Light Level:** 0
**Density:** 900
**Viscosity:** 1000

**Members**
- Unrefined Carborax
- Catalysed Carborax
- Overcharged Carborax
- Carborax Diesel
